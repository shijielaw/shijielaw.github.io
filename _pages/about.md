I'm a PhD student in Software Engineering from [School of Computer Science and Technology](https://cs.tongji.edu.cn/), [Tongji University](https://www.tongji.edu.cn/). Now my main research interest includes large language models, knowledge graph completion and reasoning. In master period, my research focused on graph data mining, graph neural networks and group link prediction.
